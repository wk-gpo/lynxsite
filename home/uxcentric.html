ux centric

UX team can work directly on the same HTML and CSS sources as the development team. UX team can work with their choice of tools, editors, CSS frameworks and libraries.
There is a continuum of UX concept-to-product process, integrated with the development process. Wireframes may be created in HTML, enabling quick move from analysis&desing to prototypes and live demos based on real content served by OSA services via Velvet interfaces.
