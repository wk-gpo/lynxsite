lynx goals & assumptions

  • easy implementation & configuration of new application regardless of the scale

    In WK ecosystem we single out two types of applications:
    • Applications from a single set of components i.e. quick assembly of new app from what’s already available.
    • And more complex, more expensive “high-end” new applications, which, once a template is complete then can be adopted widely by other businesses using configuration/parametrization tools to create new products.
    In both of cases we want to simplify and accelerate the process of application ceation.

  • end to end proccess support

    We also sngle out two types of application creation proccess:
    • From UI design, by static prototype to production application
    • From real data prototype to production application
    We promote both kinds of proccess. Lynx have to simplify and helps the application creation proccess from early begining to production deployment.

  • easy and high reusability

    Lynx have to provide the technics and paterns for creating high reusable code, within the project, bussiness and organization. The way of incresing of reusability of components have to be as simple as posible, without providing and limitations of usability.

  • agile friendly

    Especially in the aspect of:
    • early and continuous/frequent delivery,
    • embrace change,
    • inspect and adap,

  • quality and maintance

    We wants to provide solutions which halps the teams to create high quality products which will be simple to maintain and opened for change requests and impruvments
